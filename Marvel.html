Synopsis:

The series typically revolves around heroes and villains from both universes joining forces to battle a powerful antagonist threatening their worlds. The storylines vary between games, but common themes include: Marvel vs. Capcom: Clash of Super Heroes (1998) – Heroes team up to stop Apocalypse, who seeks to dominate both universes. Marvel vs. Capcom 2: New Age of Heroes (2000) – A mysterious being called Abyss threatens all fighters. Marvel vs. Capcom 3: Fate of Two Worlds (2011) – Doctor Doom and Albert Wesker join forces to conquer both dimensions, leading to an ultimate battle against Galactus. Marvel vs. Capcom: Infinite (2017) – Ultron and Sigma merge into Ultron Sigma, using the Infinity Stones to reshape reality, forcing heroes from both sides to unite. The series is known for its high-energy combat, flashy combos, and iconic character roster, making it a staple in the fighting game genre.
