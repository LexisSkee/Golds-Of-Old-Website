Synopsis:

The story revolves around a mystical martial arts tournament known as the Mortal Kombat Tournament, where fighters from Earthrealm battle warriors from other realms, such as Outworld, to prevent the invasion of Earth. The tournament is overseen by Shang Tsung, a sorcerer serving the evil emperor Shao Kahn. Iconic fighters include Scorpion, Sub-Zero, Liu Kang, Raiden, Johnny Cage, and Sonya Blade, each with unique abilities and fatalities. The game introduced mechanics like blocking, special moves, and combo attacks, as well as a deep lore spanning multiple sequels. The franchise has evolved significantly, with later titles introducing 3D gameplay, cinematic story modes, and online multiplayer, making Mortal Kombat one of the most influential and successful fighting game series in history.
