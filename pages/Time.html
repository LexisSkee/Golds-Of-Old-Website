Synopsis:

The game follows Richard Miller, an elite agent of the V.S.S.E., who is sent on a mission to rescue Rachel MacPherson, the kidnapped daughter of a powerful politician. She has been taken by Sherudo Garo, a tyrannical ruler seeking revenge. Richard must battle through waves of armed enemies, taking cover behind obstacles to avoid gunfire. The Time Crisis series is famous for its "Pedal Cover System", allowing players to hide behind cover and reload. Unlike other rail shooters, it features a time limit, requiring players to act quickly. The success of Time Crisis led to multiple sequels and spin-offs, making it one of the most iconic arcade shooting franchises in gaming history.
