METAL SLUG

Metal Slug is a run-and-gun action game series developed by SNK. It follows the battle between the Peregrine Falcon Squad—led by soldiers Marco Rossi and Tarma Roving—against the rebel forces of General Morden, who seeks world domination

Street Fighter

Street Fighter II (1991) is a classic fighting game by Capcom featuring a global martial arts tournament. Players choose from diverse fighters, each with unique moves, to battle opponents and defeat the villain, M. Bison. It revolutionized the fighting genre with combos and competitive gameplay.

Mortal Kombat

Mortal Kombat (1992) is a brutal fighting game by Midway, known for its violent combat, Fatalities, and unique characters. Fighters battle in a deadly tournament to stop the evil sorcerer Shang Tsung. Its realistic graphics and gore made it a landmark in gaming history.

Pacman

Pac-Man (1980) is an iconic arcade game by Namco. Players control Pac-Man, navigating a maze to eat pellets while avoiding ghosts. Eating power pellets lets Pac-Man temporarily consume ghosts for extra points. It became one of the most influential and recognizable video games ever.

Galaga

Galaga (1981) is a classic arcade shooter by Namco. Players control a spaceship, shooting waves of alien enemies while avoiding capture and attacks. With its challenging gameplay and iconic enemy patterns, it became one of the most popular space shooters of all time.
