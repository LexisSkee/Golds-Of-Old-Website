Synopsis:

Players control Pac-Man, a yellow, circular character who navigates a maze, eating small dots called Pac-Dots while avoiding four colorful ghosts: Blinky (Red) – Chases Pac-Man aggressively Pinky (Pink) – Tries to ambush Pac-Man Inky (Blue) – Moves unpredictably Clyde (Orange) – Sometimes chases, sometimes wanders away The goal is to clear all dots in the maze while avoiding the ghosts. Pac-Man can eat Power Pellets, which temporarily turn the ghosts blue, making them vulnerable. If he eats them, they return to their starting point. The game features increasing difficulty, with faster ghosts and complex AI patterns. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Pac-Man became a cultural phenomenon and remains one of the most recognized games in history.
