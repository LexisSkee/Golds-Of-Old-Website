Synopsis:

Metal Slug is a run-and-gun action game series developed by SNK. It follows the battle between the Peregrine Falcon Squad—led by soldiers Marco Rossi and Tarma Roving—against the rebel forces of General Morden, who seeks world domination. The series is known for its fast-paced gameplay, hand-drawn pixel art, and humor. Players fight through enemy soldiers, aliens, and other threats while using powerful weapons and piloting the iconic "Metal Slug" tank.
